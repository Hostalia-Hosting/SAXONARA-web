SAXONARA in France

As a professional saxophonist, I receive requests from different regions of France and I have played all over France, notably in parties near Toulouse and Paris, as well as in Perpignan, Lyon or Montpellier.

Events

Corporate events around French cities such as Paris, Toulouse or Montpellier, store openings or cocktails at urban penthouses are some of the events I have participated in as saxophonist in France.

Weddings

I have had the chance to visit the most spectacular estates for weddings in France, especially in the sunniest regions of the south, such as Toulouse, Perpignan or Montpellier, but also in the surroundings of Paris, playing the saxophone at the Vin d'Honneur or at the evening with the DJ and me on the saxophone.

Hotels and Clubs

In addition to private events, I also participate as a saxophonist playing in hotels, with DJs in Clubs or at Parties. Always adapting my repertoire of Electronic Music, House, Modern Pop, Disco and Jazz.
