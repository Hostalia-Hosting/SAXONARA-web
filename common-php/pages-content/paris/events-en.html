SAXONARA in Paris

As an event saxophonist, I play a lot in the same city of Paris, but also in castles and beautiful places in the South of France. Paris is the perfect place for parties and I love to participate in them as a professional saxophonist.

Events

Corporate events in Paris, store openings, cocktails at urban penthouses or boat trips on the Mediterranean are some of the events I have participated in as a saxophonist around Paris.

Weddings

Most weddings in Paris take place outside the city, in Castles and Villas. During weddings, I perform different services such as the Ceremony, the Vin d'Honneur or a memorable Evening with the DJ. If you are looking for a saxophonist for your wedding in Paris, contact me!

Hotels & Clubs

In addition to private events, I also participate as a saxophonist playing in hotels in Paris, with DJs in Clubs or at Parties. Always adapting my repertoire of Electronic Music, House, Modern Pop, Disco and Jazz.
